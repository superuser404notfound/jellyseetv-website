JellySeeTV

Your Jellyfin library and Seerr — together on Apple TV.

GitHub Privacy Impressum

JellySeeTV is a native tvOS media player for your Jellyfin server, with first-class Jellyseerr integration so you can browse and request what's missing without ever leaving the couch.

Built natively on SwiftUI, with a custom video engine handling Direct Play, HDR10, Dolby Vision and Dolby Atmos. No transcoding required for almost every codec your Apple TV understands.

Open source, end to end

JellySeeTV is MIT-licensed; the underlying video engine, AetherEngine, is LGPL-3.0. No telemetry, no analytics, no third-party SDKs phoning home. Self-host the server, audit the client.

Status

Currently in private TestFlight beta ahead of the App Store release. Source on GitHub.
