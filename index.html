Sodalite

Your Jellyfin library and Seerr, together on Apple TV.

GitHub Privacy Impressum

Sodalite is a native tvOS media player for your Jellyfin server, with first-class Jellyseerr integration so you can browse and request what's missing without ever leaving the couch.

Built natively on SwiftUI, with a custom video engine handling Direct Play, HDR10, Dolby Vision and Dolby Atmos. No transcoding required for almost every codec your Apple TV understands.

Open source, end to end

Sodalite is licensed under GPL-3.0, the underlying video engine AetherEngine under LGPL-3.0, both with an Apple Store / DRM Exception under §7 so the App Store distribution stays compatible. No telemetry, no analytics, no third-party SDKs phoning home. Self-host the server, audit the client.

Status
